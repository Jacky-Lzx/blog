Operation extension by duality

Dilation

For $\alpha > 0$, define
$$
(D_\alpha \varphi)(x) = \alpha ^{n/2} \cdot \varphi(\alpha x)
$$

The dilation operator
$$
(D_\alpha T)(\varphi) = T(D_{1/\alpha} \varphi) \
$$

$$
(T_{D_\alpha g}) = D_\alpha T_g
$$

Translation

$$
(\tau_y \varphi)(x) = \varphi(x - y)
$$

$$
(\tau_y T)(\varphi) = T(\tau_{-y} \varphi)
$$

Multiplication

$$
(M_h \varphi)(x) = h(x) \varphi(x)
$$

$$
(M_h T)(\varphi) = T(M_h \varphi)
$$

The Weak Derivative

$$
(D^\alpha T)\varphi = (-1)^{|\alpha|} T(D^\alpha \varphi)
$$

Modified Green function

$$
Lg_M(x, \xi) = \delta(x - \xi) - \sum_{i = 1}^k v^{(i)} (\xi) v^{(i)}(x), \
B_1 g_M = 0 \
\vdots \
B_2 g_M = 0 \
$$

where $v^{(1)} , \cdots, v^{(k)}$ are the $k$ orthonormalized non-trivial solutions of the completely homogeneous adjoint problem.
